Product Photography
Advertisement
 
Product Photography Advertisement
This product photography advertisement was for Hitch Hiker lemonade, who is a Canadian based drink business that make different flavours of organic lemonades. The product photography features a blue background to match the blue in the label, without distracting from the product and the fruit of the flavour. The photography is very simple to highlight the simple, natural ingredients.
Editorial Illustration
 
Editorial Illustration
This editorial illustration is based on an article about a giant octopus interacting with divers. The illustration is in a realistic-sketchy style with movement and texture, drawn digitally. The layout creates consistency and a continued image between the pages.
Blood Donation Thesis Project
 
Blood Donation Thesis Project
Bloodline is a thesis project focused on increasing blood donors to aid the blood crisis in Canada. The project features the creation of a carekit, which includes a tote bag, keychains, buttons, stickers, bandaids and an info card that are all branded to remind people to donate blood. There also is an app created to gamify blood donation, and encourage the target audience of young adults to donate.
Sustainable Packaging
Redesign
 
Sustainable Packaging Redesign
The focus of this project was to redesign the single-use packaging of a food or drink product, designing a more sustainable and ethical solution to replace it. Creating an overall more sustainable package through using upcycling and recycling. The packaging includes an aluminum box and a seed paper wrap with minimal ink coverage.
Niagara Nutrition
Partners Redesign
 
Niagara Nutrition Partners Redesign
The focus of this project was to design print and digital media for the non-profit client, Niagara Nutrition Partners. This project was a collaboration with another designer that featured the design of a bi-fold brochure, postcard, social media posts, website assets, newletter inserts and updated brand book. These designs were based off their mindset of good food is the foundation for success.
Canadian Symbol Stamps
 
Canadian Symbol Stamp
This stamp design is focused on Alberta’s provincial animal, the Rocky Mountain bighorn sheep. There is a series of four stamps, two featuring the full-colour sheep and two featuring the grey scale version. They were also expanded into a bi-fold brochure and a postcard with a big horned sheep stamp.
Interactive Bagvertising
 
Interactive Bagvertising
This interactive bag advertisement is for a plant shop in Ottawa, Canada called Plant & Curio. The idea of the bag is to create interaction between the advertisement and the customer to effetivley advertise the client. The bag design features a hanging plant illustration that blends into the handle, creating the interactive element.
The Secret of the Chimneys
Book Design
 
The Secret of the Chimneys Book Design
Agatha Christie’s book ‘The Secret of the Chimneys’ was redesigned, both cover and interior layouts. This classic mystery was turned into both a physical and digital version of the book. The illustration of the cover entices readers to open and learn of the mysteries held with in it.
